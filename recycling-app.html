Recycling App

- Service Design - Digital Art Direction -

Brief

To identify a problem that affects the environment and find a solution for it using the principles of service design.

Journey Map

The entire journey of how unused products are being disposed was mapped to give a better understanding of the process and to help identify areas that can be improved.

The Need

A platform that:
Allows users to discover new ways to recycle
Educates and motivates users about the issue
Aids the user to dispose waste responsibly
Connect users who want to trade their unused products

Introducing...

Channels

App: Where users can scan or upload the materials they want to recycle

Website: Where users can browse the uploaded products or materials

Electronic notice board: Which creates awareness about the cause and engages users to download the app

Experience Journey across channels

Final Output

Electronic Notice Baords

Electronic notice boards placed in public spaces like bus stops, metro stations etc. as well as relevant spaces like near a recycling area or large bins.
